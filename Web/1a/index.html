These are data from ~500 gene expression microarrays.

The top panel is like 500 box plots: lines are drawn at the 1, 5, 10, 25, 50, 75, 90, 95, 99 percentiles for each of 500 distributions. The distributions are sorted by their medians.

Hover over a column in the top panel and the corresponding distribution is shown below; click for it to persist; click again to make it go away.
